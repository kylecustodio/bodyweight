Minimalist Routine

A quick and simple routine for people who just want to move more and get the benefits of regular exercise.


  • The Routine
    Instructions: Complete 2-6 circuits of the following exercises. Take little to no rest. Each exercise should be done 1-2 reps short of failure.


    Exercises
    Reps Exercise
    8 Walking Lunges
    8 Push-ups
    8 Horizontal Rows
    8 Plank Shoulder Taps
