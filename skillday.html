Skill Day Routine

Reddit's beginner routine for practicing low-impact skills like the handstand and L-sit.


  • Warm Up
    Instructions: A few exercises and stretches to prepare your joints for the Skill Work. Move from one exercise to the next with little rest in between.


    Dynamic Stretches
    Reps Exercise
    10 Trap-3 Raise
    10 External Rotation
    10 Serratus Wall Slides
    30s Deadbugs
    10+ GMB Wrist Prep



  • Skill Work
    Instructions: Set a timer for 5-10 minutes and go practice your L-sits and handstand, resting as necessary. When time's up, move to the mobility work. Move on to the next progression once you can hold the position for 30s.


    Handstand Progression Level 1

    Handstand Progression

    Level 1: Wall Plank Level 2: Wall Handstand Level 3: Freestanding Handstand
    Cancel
    Reps Exercise
    5-10min Wall Plank


    L-sit Progression Level 1

    L-sit Progression

    Level 1: Foot Supported L-sit Level 2: One-Leg Foot Supported L-sit Level 3: Elevated Tuck L-sit Level 4: Tuck L-sit Level 5: Advanced Tuck L-sit Level 6: L-sit
    Cancel
    Reps Exercise
    2-3min Foot Supported L-sit
